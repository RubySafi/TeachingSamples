Transformations

Let's learn how to move and change shapes using transformations! We will look at translation, rotation, dilation, scaling, and reflection.

Section 1: What do they mean?

Here is a quick guide to the words we use for changing shapes.

Terminology Popular Name Meaning Figure
Translation Slide Moving a shape to a new place without turning it.
Rotation Turn Spinning a shape around a center point.
abc
Dilation Enlargement / Reduction Making a shape bigger or smaller while keeping the same shape.
abc
Reflection Flip Flipping a shape over a line, like looking in a mirror.
Scaling Resizing Changing the size of a shape (sometimes making it wider or taller).
