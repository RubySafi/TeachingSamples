Derived Units: Building New Units

Just like building with LEGO blocks, we can combine simple units like meters (m), kilograms (kg), and seconds (s) to create new "Derived Units."

Section 1: The Connection Rules

We use two main symbols to connect units:

Operation Symbol Example How to read
Multiplication (×) · (dot) m · kg "meter-kilograms"
Division (÷) / (slash) m / s "meters per second"

💡 Note: If you divide by many things, we use parentheses to keep them together:
m / (kg · s)
