1. Understanding Fractions

A fraction a/b means "a divided by b". For example, 3/4 represents three parts out of four equal parts.

2. Equivalent Fractions

If you multiply or divide both the numerator and the denominator by the same number, the value of the fraction does not change.

Example: 2/3 = 4/6 = 6/9
